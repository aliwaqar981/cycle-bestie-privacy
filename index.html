Cycle Bestie App Privacy Policy

Welcome to Cycle Bestie! Your privacy is paramount to us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

Information Collection

When you use the Cycle Bestie app, we collect personal information that you provide, such as your name, email address, and health-related data that you choose to enter. We use this information to enhance your experience and provide personalized insights and recommendations.

Use of Information

Your information helps us to improve our app and customer service. It enables us to provide a personalized experience and support your wellness journey effectively.

Communication

By creating an account, you agree that we may send you emails about your account, our services, and occasional promotional offers. You can unsubscribe from these communications at any time through the unsubscribe link in the emails.

Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your personal information is contained behind secured networks and is only accessible by a limited number of persons who have special access rights to such systems.

Non-Selling of Information

We value your trust and assure you that your information will not be sold to any third parties.

Changes to this Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via email at support@cyclebestie.com.

Thank you for choosing Cycle Bestie!
